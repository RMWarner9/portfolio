Ramona's Bio

About Ramona:

Ramona was an inspirational woman. Taking on the role of raising both of her children as a single mother, she did everything she could to provide for them. In her early years, she ran track and field. She worked for the U.S. Attorney's office as an IT Specialist for 30 years before her retirement.
